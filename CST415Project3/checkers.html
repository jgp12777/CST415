Game Rules & Algorithm Details:

Multi-Jump Rule: When you capture an opponent's piece, if another jump is available with the same piece, you MUST continue jumping. This creates chain captures!

Difficulty Levels: Beginner (depth 1, 40% random moves), Easy (depth 2), Medium (depth 4), Hard (depth 6). Higher depths evaluate exponentially more positions.

Minimax with Alpha-Beta Pruning: The AI explores future game states and chooses moves that maximize its advantage while minimizing yours.

Evaluation Function: Considers piece count and king value. Higher difficulties also factor in position, advancement, and board control.
